Words

Upekkha
Origin: Pali
Meaning: Equanimity, the ability to maintain calm and serenity in the midst of difficulties.
Sati
Origin: Pali
Meaning: Mindfulness, the practice of being fully present in the current moment without judgment.
Karuna
Origin: Pali
Meaning: Compassion, the deep empathy and desire to alleviate the suffering of others.
Anicca
Origin: Pali
Meaning: Impermanence, the understanding that all things are in constant change and nothing is permanent.
Dukkha
Origin: Pali
Meaning: Suffering or unsatisfactoriness, the inherent experience of life including pain, stress, and discontent.
